# sanban
Chōsho wa
Watashi wa charenji o toru koto to,
mondai o kaiketsu suru koto to
atarashii koto o manabu koto ga suki desu.
Mata, atarashii kankyō ni mo sugu nareru koto ga dekimasu.
Atarashii koto o hayaku oboeru koto ga dekimasu.
Soshite, watashi wa itsumo isshōkenmei ganbatte iru hito desu.

Sono kekka wa, daigaku de wa sotsugyō
suru toki ni ichiban takai seiseki o toru koto ga dekimashita.
Mata, IT no kenshū o iroiro shūryō shinagara, daigaku no gakuseikai katsudō ni mo sanka shimashita.

Watashi no tansho wa, ōzei no hito no mae de hanasu toki ni kinchō shimasu.
Shikashi, itsumo akiramezu ni charenji o tsuzukete imasu.


# yonban
Watashi wa bakkuendo, furontoendo, shisutemu kaihatsu,
kuraudo, dētabēsu no junia
reberu no chishiki to keiken ga arimasu.

Korekara, motto benkyō shite sukiru o migakitai desu.
Soshite, kōhinshitsu na shisutemu o tsukutte,
jōzu na enjinia ni naritai desu.
