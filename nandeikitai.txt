#ichiban
Watashi wa shōgakusei no koro kara ima made, 
zutto Nihon ga daisuki desu.
Nihon o shitta kikkake wa anime deshita. 
Sono ato, Nihon ni tsuite samazama na koto o manabi,
Nihon to iu kuni ni masumasu kyōmi o motsu yō ni narimashita.

# niban
Watashi ni totte Nihon wa, hitobito
ga otagai ni reigi tadashiku, totemo 
yuuniku to sumiyasui kuni desu. 
Mata, utsukushii shizen ya samazama na bunka ga ari, 
totemo miryokuteki da to omoimasu. Sarani, 
Nihon nara de wa no fun'iki mo totemo suki desu.

Sono tame, watashi wa hoka no kuni de wa naku, 
Nihon de hataraku koto o mokuhyō ni shiteimasu.

# sanban
Shikashi, watashi ni totte ichiban taisetsu na no wa, 
shakai no yaku ni tatsu hito ni naru koto desu. 
IT no bun'ya o tōshite, 
Nihon no kigyō ya shakai ni kōken shitai to kangaeteimasu.
